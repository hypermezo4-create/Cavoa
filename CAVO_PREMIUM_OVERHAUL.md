# CAVO Premium Overhaul

## Implemented in this update

1. Global premium visual system with reusable glass/background widgets.
2. Stronger bottom navigation with floating glass bar and cart badge.
3. Full home screen redesign with cinematic hero, 3D-style headline, richer category cards, and upgraded featured grid.
4. Categories screen redesign with category chips, brand filters, search shell, and premium product grid.
5. Product details redesign with large hero gallery, bigger pricing section, better size selector, benefits blocks, and sticky add-to-cart bar.
6. Full-screen product gallery viewer with swipe + pinch zoom + HD-focused presentation.
7. Cart redesign with larger item cards, cleaner summary, and premium checkout handoff.
8. Checkout redesign with stronger sectioning, payment method cards, screenshot upload state, and WhatsApp order handoff.
9. Order creation now writes into OrderController before WhatsApp handoff.
10. Profile redesign with member card, stats, appearance/language controls, and recent orders section.
11. Links page redesign with stronger brand presentation and native-color social cards.
12. Upgraded network image loading placeholders.

## Notes

- Existing project structure and core flows were preserved.
- Payment screenshot requirement is still present in this version to avoid removing current behavior.
- This update is focused on premium UI/UX polish and structural presentation.
